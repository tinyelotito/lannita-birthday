Forever yours,
Your Mom

For You, Mi Flower Moon

Usually, I would have written a poem, but it's hard to describe everything in a poem. So I am using prose, having hope that I can at least make a small part of all the ideas and feelings I have clear (even though I don't think I'll succeed.) Also, poetry is for hopeless dreamers, but we are realists, aren't we?


Hope

like the key to paradise

if you own it

you're saved

But we enter paradise

in our dreams

and when we die.

I made sure to understand this clearly. I tied it to my heart and my mind, for if any of them failed to remember the other will remind this body. But you have proven to me that heavens can come to earth, that pessimism is not the ideal state, and that hope is not a treason. That the key can open the doors in our life without a lot of burdens.


Since I have met you, a radical change has happened to me. I gave up on my past state. I have come to be more cheerful and optimistic about life. I have come to rethink love as a sweet thing.


Today, you are the flower moon. For you the flowers have risen and adorned the earth with beauty and sweet fragrance. For you today Selena has stranded herself away in her orbit, for your shine has covered her. Today, I am reminded of your light and the darkness blinding me before we met. They are all I need to remember you. Today I am reminded of your love adorning my days with cherish and joy.


Despite all the challenges we may face, I wish we always stay together, forever in tranquility and joy. And wish we have all our adventures together. Always there by your side and showing you all the love I am capable of.


Happy Birthday Mi Flower Moon


Forever yours and by your side,
Ibrahim (Elotito)
